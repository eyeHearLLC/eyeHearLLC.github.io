Privacy Policy

eyeHear allows anyone with an iPhone to caption any conversation anywhere.

Press and hold iPhone's screen to caption what you or anyone else is saying in large, easy-to-read text.

eyeHear was designed to enable easier conversation for the Deaf and Hard-of-Hearing communities in situations where sign language is not an option.

Best part: eyeHear is free to download and use.
